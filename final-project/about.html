The MovieBox Story

Founded in 2026, MovieBox started as a final project in BYU-I WDD 231 as something that interest me, and wil alow me demonstrate what I have learned in the Course. I realized that with thousands of movies releasing every year, finding high-quality, curated information was becoming harder than ever.

Our goal is to provide a seamless, ad-free experience where you can browse upcoming blockbusters, read deep-dive descriptions, and manage your personal watchlist with a single click.

Curated Database

We focus on quality over quantity, highlighting the most anticipated 2025 and 2026 releases.

User First

Your watchlist is stored locally, ensuring your data stays private and accessible instantly.

Fast & Fluid

Built with performance in mind, MovieBox provides a lightning-fast browsing experience.
