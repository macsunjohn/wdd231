About Me

Hello! My name is Mark John, and I am currently studying Software Development at Brigham Young University-Idaho (BYU-Idaho), USA. I am based in Abuja, Nigeria, where I continue to build my knowledge and skills in modern technology and software development. I have a strong passion for writing code and creating digital solutions. My interests include surfing the internet to explore new technologies, playing basketball, and traveling to experience new environments and ideas. My long-term goal is to continue developing software even at old age, constantly learning and contributing to the tech community throughout my life. Over time, I have developed skills in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, SQL, Power BI, AI automation, and social media operations. I enjoy combining these skills to build useful and innovative digital projects. I am always eager to learn new technologies, collaborate with others, and improve my abilities as a software developer.
